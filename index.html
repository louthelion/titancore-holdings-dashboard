Central Connection Flow

Step 1: Work is handled inside TitanCore Holdings.

Step 2: Inbox, approvals, meetings, signatures, investor work, institution work, and financial work are organized here.

Step 3: Monthly, quarterly, six-month, and year-end reports are prepared here.

Step 4: Finalized company reports are sent to the Central Dashboard.

Step 5: CEO, President, and central leadership can enter from the Central Dashboard and review the full TitanCore Holdings work flow.
