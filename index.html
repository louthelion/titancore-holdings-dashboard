Reporting Cycle

Monday: All companies send reports, complaints, finance, contract issues, and updates to TitanCore Holdings.

Monday all day: TitanCore Holdings Manager, Assistant Manager, and Administrator prepare the package.

Tuesday morning: TitanCore Holdings sends the package to the General Manager, General Assistant Manager, and General Administrator.

Wednesday afternoon: General team holds the big review meeting, including TitanCore Holdings company management.

Thursday morning: General team sends the full information upward to the Mantle of 7.

Friday afternoon: Mantle of 7 meets and decides future direction for all companies.

Saturday: Mantle of 7 meets with the General team and hands down required actions for each company.
